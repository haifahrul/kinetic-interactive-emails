A) Introduction to Kinetic - top

Kinetic is first ever interactive email templates set of Themeforest. This set comes with 3 very unique templates that feature an interactive carousel, progressive disclosure expendable module and an advanced video email.

In this set you will find the following templates:

  • Kinetic
  • Kinetic Blog
  • Kinetic Video

When combined with Robust Designs Template Editor, you can make Unlimited Combination of designs to use for whichever purpose you want.

All templates in this set are Fully Customizable in Robust Designs Template Editor and also Fully Supported in many Email Service Provides including MailChimp, Campaign Monitor, Active Campaign, Zoho Campaigns and more mentioned below.

 

All templates included in this set are backed by our design guarantee. You can trust us with reliability you deserve.

B) Template Editor and How-To Edit - top

By purchasing this product you have gained full access to 

Robust Design Template Editor features Save and Edit later, Unlimited Colors, EASY Inline-Editing, Module Specific Editing and many more features to make your editing experience as smooth as possible.

To see a demo of our editor, head over to the templates page and select a template to be redirected to the Editor.

Once you've finished editing, you can click on the download button on the top-left of the screen to open a menu where you can optimize your template for either MailChimp, MadMimi, Campaign Monitor or plain (by unticking all optimization options) and input your purchase code to download your updated and optimized template.

E) How-To Find Purchase Code - top

To find your licence key/purchase code you need to log into your ThemeForest account and go to your "Downloads" page.

Locate some of the RobustDesigns themes you purchased in your "Downloads" list and click on the Download and next on the "License Certificate/purchase code" link in drop-down menu.

This is what a License Certificate document looks like, where you’ll find the item purchase code inside.
